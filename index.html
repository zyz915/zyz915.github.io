Research

I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Informatics, SOKENDAI (The Graduate University for Advanced Studies). I obtained my Master's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

My research interest includes parallel and distributed graph algorithms and solutions for large-scale graph processing. My recent research projects focus on the vertex-centric graph processing (VcGP) systems, including the Palgol language providing intuitive high-level programming interface for VcGP, the Pregel-channel graph system for composing optimizations in VcGP, and an experimental SQL-like language for detecting suitable optimizations through compilation.
